Biography

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering at the Pabna University of Science and Technology (PUST). I am also a registered professional engineer in Bangladesh. My current research includes seismic performance and vulnerability assessment, seismic hazard and risk assessment, structural health monitoring, soil-structure interaction, primary-secondary structure interaction, and the development of computer tools for civil engineering applications.

I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering at Kunsan National University, South Korea, in 2022, and my B.Sc. from Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology (RUET) in 2012.
